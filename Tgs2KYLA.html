What's Rose?

Rose is a type of flower known for its round shape, sweet aroma, various colors, and sharp thorns lining its stem. Roses are often regarded as symbols of love, affection, and beauty, and are frequently used in special occasions or as gifts to express emotions. Furthermore, rose oil is also used in the perfume and cosmetics industry due to its distinctive fragrance, making it one of the most famous and cherished flowers worldwide.
